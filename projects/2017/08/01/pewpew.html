Pew Pew (code)

pico8

‹ ›

    Pew Pew is a fast paced local competitive multiplayer game inspired by Asteroids.

    I developed Pew Pew using pico8 (which is a fantasy console with substantial constraints tied to memory, graphics, sounds, etc.) while working at Borealy Games. I was inspired to work on this project by Dominique Morin who was truly a pleasure to work with and impressed me greatly with his portfolio of games.
