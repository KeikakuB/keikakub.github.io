    "Billguk the Invasion Logistics Professional is a arcade-style highscore game in which you're tasked invasion pods launched from our underground bunkers towards the surface of the Earth while avoiding dangerous zones."

    Billguk the Invasion Logistics Professional was developed using Unity for Ludum Dare 29 (theme: Beneath the Surface).
